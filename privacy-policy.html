Policy statement

1. Information we collect

Tiger Tap - Jogo Tigrinho may collect limited non-personal and technical data through third-party analytics and attribution services, including Firebase Analytics and AppsFlyer.

This may include app activity and usage data, such as app launches, screen views, session duration, interactions with app features, and in-app events.

The app may also process device or other identifiers, such as advertising identifiers, app instance identifiers, device identifiers, or similar technical identifiers.

2. Approximate location and technical data

Approximate location may be derived from technical information such as IP address for analytics, attribution, fraud prevention, regional reporting, and marketing measurement purposes.

Technical data may include device type, operating system version, app version, language, country or region, and diagnostic information.

3. Information we do not intentionally collect

We do not intentionally collect directly identifying personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or payment information through the app.

4. How we use data

Collected data may be used for analytics, measuring app usage, understanding how users interact with the app, improving performance and stability, marketing attribution, advertising campaign measurement, fraud prevention, security, and compliance.

5. Third-party services and data sharing

The app may use third-party services including Firebase Analytics by Google and AppsFlyer. These services may collect, receive, process, and store data according to their own privacy policies and terms.

Usage data, app activity data, device identifiers, approximate location, and technical data may be shared with or processed by third-party analytics, attribution, advertising, marketing measurement, and fraud prevention providers.

We do not sell personal information. However, certain data may be processed by third-party service providers as described in this Privacy Policy.

6. Data security

Data transmitted from the app to third-party services is encrypted in transit using secure protocols such as HTTPS/TLS.

Although reasonable security measures are used, no method of transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

7. Data retention

Collected data may be retained by third-party service providers according to their own data retention policies and applicable legal requirements.

8. User data deletion

At this time, Tiger Tap - Jogo Tigrinho does not provide a direct in-app mechanism or separate request process for users to request deletion of analytics, attribution, or device identifier data.

Accordingly, in the Google Play Data Safety section, the answer to whether users can request data deletion is: No.

9. Children’s privacy

Tiger Tap - Jogo Tigrinho is not intended to knowingly collect directly identifying personal information from children. The app may collect limited usage data, app activity data, device identifiers, approximate location, and technical data through third-party analytics and attribution services as described above.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us at the email address listed in this policy.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published clearly on this page.
